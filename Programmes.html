Our Programs

Navchetna Summer Camp

In this camp more than 100 children participated and learned a lot of skills. Our aim was to promote learning by doing with joy and creativity. There were 10 active volunteers who supported to make it possible, in future we plan to have a series of navchetna summer camps.

Pitara Quiz

Pitara quiz is a non competitive quiz where everyone who participates is a winner, through the quiz we are working to remove hesitation from children and enhance their drawing skill.

Art Festival

In this festival we cover all kind of arts like creative writing, painting craft, origami or making something from out of waste, the art festival is loved by all children. This is done in different communities on regular basis.

Film Festival

Our film festival program doesn't promote main stream entertainment, we screen documentaries on various issues, our main focus for the film festival is to make the youth, women, children and community members aware. So we choose such topics which are associated with social issues.

Kitchen Garden

We believe that everyone should grow his/her own food, with this aim we collaborate with schools and try to help set a diverse kitchen garden. Even in local communities we promote organic and natural farming, we share the knowledge of permaculture, indigenous seeds, indigenous composting and more.

Theatre Workshop

We do theatre activities with children. It is our experience that kids who participate in theatre become vocal and their oratory skills reach at next level. It also creates a sense of joy and fun during classes, children enjoy a lot in the drama series. What we do includes Balgeet and expressions.

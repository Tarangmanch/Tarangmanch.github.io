Our Programs

Navchetna Summer Camp

In this camp more than 100 children participated and learn a lot of skills. Our aim was to promote learning by doing with joy and creativity. There were 10 active volunteers who supported to make it possible, in future we are planning to have series of navchetna summer camp.

Pitara Quiz

Pitara quiz is a non competitive quiz everyone who participate is a winner, through this quiz we are working to remove hesitation from children and enhance their drawing skill.

Art Festival

In this festival we cover all kind of arts like creative writing, painting craft, origami or making something from out of waste, the art festival is loved by all children. We do it in different different communities on regular basis.

Film Festival

Our film festival program doesn't promote main stream entertainment, we screen documentaries on various issues, our main focus for the film festival is to make aware youth, women, children and community members. So we choose such topic which are associated with social issues.

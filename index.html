Transforming Education with Creative arts.
Tarang manch is an active youth group based in Allahganj, Shahjahanpur, who believes that quality education should be available for everyone regardless of social, cultural and economic differences we are born into.
Our youth training program in collaboration with various organizations is impacting in leadership development. We are working with 500 children, 20 teachers and 40 adolescent girls.

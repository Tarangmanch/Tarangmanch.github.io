About Us

What Do We Do?

Transforming Education with Creative arts. Tarang manch is an active youth group based in Allahganj, Shahjahanpur, who believe that quality education should be available for everyone regardless of social, cultural and economic differences we are born into.

We work with private and government schools through various creative interventions like theatre, Music, Farming, arts and crafts to foster the spirit of learning by doing.

Throughout the year we organize summers camp, film festival, bird watching and nature connect activities, awareness campaign and much more. Our youth training program in collaboration with various organizations is impacting in leadership development of various individuals and groups.

We are currently engaged in working with 500 children, 20 teachers and 40 adolescent girls on regular basis.

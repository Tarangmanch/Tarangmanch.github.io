About Us

What Do We Do?

Transforming Education with Creative arts. Tarang manch is a group of youth who believe that quality education should be available for everyone. We are working with Private and Govt. Schools through various activities like theater, farming craft and arts. On a regular basis we do events like summer camp, film festival, bird watching, awareness campaign and more. Our youth training program in collaboration with various organizations is impacting in leadership development. We are working with over 500 children, 20 teachers and 40 adolescent girls.
